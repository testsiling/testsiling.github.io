Our Work: Diabetes Insurance Guide

This is an incompleted, unprofessional, buggy web app.

Project Introduction

My Devpost Profile

Github Repo

In conclusion, we did an awful job. I am not complaining. I am reviewing. We grouped up through the Devpost forum. We are all good at different fields, such as robotic stuff, JAVA, business, and data(me). The point is we haven’t brought out an idea that can integrate our strength. Web app is a suck idea. Also, none of us is diabetes, we are not familiar with the users’ needs.

I am responsible for the database modeling, back-end functionalities, and all php codes. They even don’t know how to test a web app. Most of them haven’t got any experience on database design and data manipulation. That is why I said web app is a bad idea.

Overall, this is a good chance to improve my communication skills.

See you next year.
