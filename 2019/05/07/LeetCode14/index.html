    200. Number of Islands (Med)

    DFS Solution

    Traverse all cell, meets island, res++. Mark the visited island as 0, then go into 4 direction from that island.

    when reach the edge or reach water, go back.

    BFS

    traverse all cell. If meets island, explore its neighbours island and log into a queue. Then neighbours’ neighbour until all neighbors found.
